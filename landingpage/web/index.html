FAQ

Common questions

How much does each video cost to produce?

A typical 12–18 minute biblical chapter runs around $5–7 in API costs (FLUX images, Kling motion, ElevenLabs narration, JSON2Video assembly). Shorter custom scripts can run as low as $2–3.

How long does a render take?

Most videos finish in 12–18 minutes. Long chapters (over 900 words) are auto-split into two parts so they keep moving even on slower scenes. You can stop the render mid-flight to save credits and resume from where it stopped.

What output formats can I download?

Pick the aspect ratio before you render: 16:9 (YouTube), 1:1 (Instagram feed), or 9:16 (Reels, Shorts, TikTok). Every render is delivered as a clean MP4 with word-level subtitles.

Who owns the video?

You do. Anointed never claims rights to your output. Upload it to YouTube, sell it, license it, embed it in a sermon — it's yours.
